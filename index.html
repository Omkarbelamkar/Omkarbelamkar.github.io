Welcome to Sansmaran

Your dedicated digital companion for spiritual growth, personal wellness, and mindful living. This manual is designed to guide you seamlessly through the app's diverse ecosystem.

  • Track your daily spiritual disciplines (Sadhana).
  • Access a rich repository of media and scriptures.
  • Connect with community circles and volunteer.
  • Utilize specialized inner-growth and utility tools.

1. Today's Practice & Daily Sadhana

Your primary workspace centers around tracking your spiritual disciplines. Sansmaran simplifies monitoring daily targets to keep you consistently connected to your goals.

The Home Dashboard

  • Personalized Greeting: Real-time greetings and scripture quotes.
  • Today's Sadhana: Total Japas logged against your target.
  • My Goals: Progress of your weekly target habits.

Tracking Japa & Routine

Tap "Daily Sadhana" on your home screen to open the counter page. Use the big [+] and [-] buttons for precision tracking (e.g., "On Mala 1, Bead 0 / 108").

2. Habit Formation & Physical Wellness

True spiritual wellness respects the integration of mind, spirit, and body. The Habits and Exercise panels keep your lifestyle rhythm balanced.

Habits Tracker

Log essential traits on a recurring weekly timeline:

  • ध्यान (Meditation)
  • व्यायाम (Minimal Exercise)
  • सात्त्विक आहार (Sattvic Diet)
  • अखंड गुरुनाम (Guru Remembrance)

Body Metrics & Hydration

Yoga Mode: Tailored tracking for Guided Meditation, Yogasana, and Sukshma Yoga.

BMI Calculator: Input weight and height to get your health index.

Water Tracker: Quick instant logging buttons (+150ml, +250ml, +500ml) right on the home dashboard.
